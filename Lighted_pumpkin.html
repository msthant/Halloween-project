Jack-O'-Lantern

img from- HollidaySmart

Jack-o’-lantern is made with pumpkin and the candle inside. People have been making jack-o’-lanterns at Halloween for centuries. Its name came from ‘Jack of the lantern’ and later changed into simply ‘Jack -o’- lantern'. Now, people decorate their house with them during Halloween nights.
