Halloween Costumes

Jock Jibberish / wjez.com

Now a day, Halloween is very popular and many children and adults celebrate Halloween by different types of ways and wearing costumes.
Halloween costumes are often designed to imitate supernatural and scary beings. Costumes are traditionally those of monsters such as vampires, werewolves, zombies, ghosts, skeletons, witches and devils or in more recent years such science fiction-inspired characters as aliens and superheroes. This is really fun and people like to wear these costumes during Halloween.
